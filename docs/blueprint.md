# **App Name**: Culinary Canvas

## Core Features:

- Account Creation: Allow customers to create and manage their profiles, including setting calorie targets and food preferences.
- Slot Booking: Enable customers to view available cooking slots and book them according to their convenience.
- Preference-Based Recommendation: Suggest cooking slots and meal ideas based on the customer's specified dietary needs, preferences, and calorie target.
- Interactive Calendar View: Visually display booked and available slots on an interactive calendar.
- Automated Reminders: Send automated reminders to customers about their upcoming cooking slots and booked meals.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A warm terracotta (#E07A5F) to evoke feelings of warmth and home cooking.
- Background color: Light beige (#F8F5F1), a desaturated tone from the primary, to create a clean, inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Olive green (#81B29A), an analogous color, to highlight important elements such as CTAs.
- Font pairing: 'Belleza' for headings and 'Alegreya' for body text.
- Use minimalist line icons representing various cooking and food-related actions and preferences.
- A clean, grid-based layout that emphasizes readability and easy navigation.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
